Jinjo Collectors

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four jinjos below. By clicking on a jinjo you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the given random number,you love the game should your total score go above the random number.

The value of each jinjo is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of the jinjos change.
